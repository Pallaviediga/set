Tourism Places

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is renowned for its stunning architecture and is a symbol of eternal love.

Mysore palace

The Mysore Palace, located in Karnataka, is a grand royal residence known for its Indo-Saracenic architecture. It is beautifully illuminated at night and attracts millions of visitors every year.

Char Minar

Charminar is a historic monument located in Hyderabad, built in 1591 by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah. It is renowned for its four grand arches and beautiful Islamic architecture.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Harmandir Sahib, is a stunning Sikh shrine located in Amritsar, Punjab. Its gold-covered structure and serene setting reflect spiritual peace and devotion.
